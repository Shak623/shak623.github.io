Mist Network Graphs

Mist is an interscholastic tournament where high school students collaborate and compete, develop creative skills, and learn from professionals, while educating them about Islam and Muslims. Their programming includes annual regional and national tournaments in more than a dozen regions across North America.

I was tasked with taking data from people participating in MIST and showing their connections to their friends in an interactive graph. The graphs were generated using d3.js and they were taken from various states and citites within America. These include Chicago, Dallas, DC, New Jersey, and New York.

The data was taken from two different time periods: before MIST and after MIST. The objective was to see if people would gain connections to friends after MIST. However, the results of this were inconclusive. The interactive graphs are provided below:
